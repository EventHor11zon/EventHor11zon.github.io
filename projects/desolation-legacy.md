# Description
* DESOLATION tells the story of a 23-year-old worker named Aiden Williams, waking up from a long hibernation in an **outpost** located on a **resource-rich asteroid**. The outpost, which was **abandoned** 50 years ago, is not safe anymore, as the robots' mechanisms were fried by the Sun, thus being **hostile** to Aiden. Through various threats like robots and obstacles, Aiden has to make his way to a launchpad to **escape the outpost**, all while questioning **why** he "woke up" in the first place. Many relevations await the one who is awake.
* Besides the story, DESOLATION also offers **two extra game modes** to play: _Infinite_ and _Playground_. Infinite mode is all about surviving as long as possible while fighting waves of enemies, and Playground mode lets the player do whatever they want using the materials given and with console commands.
